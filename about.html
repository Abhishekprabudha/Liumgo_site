About Liumgo

Building the EV‑native operating system for city logistics

Liumgo was created around a simple idea: last‑mile in Indian cities needs to be electric‑first, software‑defined and brutally reliable — not just cheaper.

Our teams come from logistics, mobility and technology. We understand how chaotic urban delivery can get — peak‑hour traffic, gated communities, rider churn, weather events, compliance obligations and more. Instead of treating these as edge cases, we design for them from day zero.

Every new lane we open runs through the same playbook: ground mapping, partner calibration, EV suitability, digital workflows and clear SLAs. That is how we keep service quality high while keeping emissions and per‑drop costs under control.

For clients, this translates into three things: fewer escalations, cleaner ESG reporting and a logistics partner that behaves like an extension of their own team.
