What Lium Go delivers

A single EV-first operations layer that plugs into your existing order, warehouse and transport systems.

Urban last-mile delivery

Structured EV routes for same-day and next-day deliveries from warehouses, micro-fulfilment centres and dark stores into dense city clusters.

  • Slot-based and wave-based dispatch models
  • Optimised routing for traffic, distance and battery levels
  • Configurable SLAs per lane and store

Dedicated fleets for enterprises

Dedicated EV fleets with co-branded riders and vehicles, managed under a single governance framework for your geography or brand.

  • Shared control tower metrics and daily scorecards
  • Uniform SOPs across partners and hubs
  • Capacity aligned to campaign and seasonality peaks

Temperature-controlled & critical flows

EV-led deliveries for pharma, healthcare and high-value B2B flows with continuous telemetry and exception handling.

  • Device and shipment level tracking hooks
  • Escalation trees for time-sensitive shipments
  • Audit-ready logs for compliance teams

Network design & EV advisory

Joint design of hub locations, service areas and charging strategy to ensure EV economics work at scale.

  • Lane-by-lane cost and service modelling
  • Charging and swapping strategy design
  • Pilot-to-scale playbooks for each vertical

EV network engineered for uptime, not experiments

Lium Go treats EV logistics as infrastructure, not just a fleet. Charging, maintenance, routing and rider utilisation are coordinated centrally so you don't carry technology or operations risk.

Every new city is onboarded through a structured readiness checklist: lanes, partners, riders, SLAs, charging partners and data integrations.

  • Charging time tracked as a first-class constraint
  • Emission baselines and savings reports for sustainability teams
